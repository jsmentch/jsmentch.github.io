## Project Snapshot

This project used immersive 360 degree scenes, high-resolution eye-tracking, and salience modeling to study how people allocate attention in naturalistic visual environments. The broader research connected gaze behavior, perceptual load, visual salience, and autism-relevant behavioral markers.
